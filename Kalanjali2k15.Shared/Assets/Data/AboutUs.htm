Sir M Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology ,Bengaluru has been conducting Kalanjali, an annual inter collegiate Techno-Cultural Fest successfully since the year 1996.

Kalanjali has been setting the stage for the student community to showcase their talent ,innovation and creativity with zeal and zest.Kalanjaili 2015 is scheduled to be held on 16th October and 17th October 2015.
